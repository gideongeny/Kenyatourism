# Privacy Policy for Tembea Kenya

**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

Tembea Kenya ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Location Data
To provide real-time weather information for travel destinations, our app uses GPS coordinates (Latitude and Longitude) of the destinations listed. We do **not** track your personal real-time location. The coordinates used are strictly for the purpose of fetching weather data from our service provider.

### Advertising
Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as:
- IP Address
- Advertising ID (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
- Device identifiers
- App usage information

For more information, please visit [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## 2. Third-Party Services
Tembea Kenya integrates the following third-party services:
- **Open-Meteo**: Used for fetching real-time weather data. No personal data is shared with Open-Meteo other than the destination coordinates.
- **Google AdMob**: Used for monetization and analytics.

## 3. Data Retention
We do not store any personal user data on our servers. Any preferences (like your Favorites list) are stored locally on your device.

## 4. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the app, but please be aware that no security measure is perfect.
